Taco Soup

About this Recipe

This delicous soup is a great lunch or dinner for the whole family.

This recipe is also super customizable, you can add tostitos, lettuce, cheese, and my personal favorite,cream cheese!

The best part of this recipe is how simple it is to cook. The ingredients are cheap and simple to put together. Not to mention the leftovers that are just as tasty reheated.

Ingredients

  • 1lb Ground Beef
  • 1/2 cup of Heavy Whipping Cream
  • 8oz Block of Cream Cheese, softened
  • 2 1/2 cups of Beef Stock
  • 1 packet of Taco Seasoning
  • 1 tsp Garlic Powder
  • 2 cans of Rotel
  • 1 medium Onion, diced

Cooking Instructions

  1. Take out the cream cheese to soften
  2. Dice your onion and put it in a pot with the ground beef over medium heat. Wait until the beef is browned and drain the grease.
  3. Once the beef is drained, return it to the pot and add the cream cheese. Stir the cream cheese until it is completely dissolved into the meat and onions.
  4. Add the rest of the ingredients and simmer over medium heat for 30 minutes.
  5. Remove from heat and enjoy with your favorite taco topping!
