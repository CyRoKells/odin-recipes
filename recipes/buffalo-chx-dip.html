Buffalo Chicken Dip

An easy snack food recipe for people that love a little spice!

Whether its a football game, party, or ordinary day, the buffalo chicken dip is always a wonderful snack to reach out to. You can eat the dip with anything you like. Chicken in a Biscuit crackers are my personal favorite to add a little extra flavor to your tastebuds! With only 5 different ingredients and a simple 20 minutees in the oven, buffalo chicken dip is always a great go-to snack!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups canned chicken
  • 8 oz of cream cheese
  • 3/4 cups of Franks Hot Sauce
  • 3/4 cups of Ranch dressing
  • 1/2 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese

Cooking Instructions

  1. Soften the cream cheese
  2. Mix everything except for the mozzarella cheese
  3. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese on top.
  4. Bake at 350 degrees fahrenheit for 20 minutes> or until the cheese is slightly browned.
