Peanut Butter Blossoms

These cookies are a holdiday favorite of mine. So much so that I don't even save then strictly for the holidays, but they do make nice holiday gifts!

I remember making these cookies with my mom years and years ago. I always loved to roll the doughball into the sugar and to stick the kiss on top when they were out of the oven. Sometimes the peanut butter is just not enough and I'll stick a Reese's cup in place of the Hershey's kiss.

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cups of flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cups of light brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp of milk
  • 1 stick of butter
  • 1/2 cup of smooth peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup of granulated sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Cooking Instructions

  1. Sift flour, baking soda, and salt. Set aside in a bowl.
  2. Cream the butter, peanut butter, sugar, and brown sugar together.
  3. Add egg, milk, and vanilla extract. Beat until blended.
  4. Slowly add the flour mix and mix thoroughly.
  5. If soft, refridgerate for 1 hour.
  6. Roll into 1" balls and roll them in a bowl of granulated sugar.
  7. Place on a greased baking sheet 2" apart.
  8. Bake at 375 degrees fahrenheit for 6-8 minutes or until lightly browned.
  9. Once removed from the oven, add a Hershey Kiss in the center of each cookie.
